Our Story

A house.
A feeling.
A ritual.

Ruma 52 was born from a simple idea: what if your favourite cafe felt exactly like home? We took an old house and turned it into a place where time slows down — where the coffee is made with care, the food is honest and good, and the atmosphere holds you just right.

Every corner was chosen deliberately. The paper lanterns, the Eames chairs, the warm light through the blinds — all of it designed to make you stay a little longer than you planned.

The Space

Come as you are.
Stay as long as you like.

"Every corner was placed with intention."

Why Ruma 52

More than a cafe.
A feeling.

We curate every detail — from the beans in your cup to the light through the blinds — so you always leave feeling better than when you arrived.

01
Specialty Coffee

Sourced carefully. Brewed with precision. Whether you're a filter devotee or a milk-coffee purist, we've got your cup.

02
Curated Ambience

Paper lanterns, mid-century chairs, warm blinds, a vinyl spinning in the corner. The space is the experience.

03
Honest Food

Freshly baked pastries and handcrafted drinks made daily. Nothing frozen. Nothing rushed. Everything intentional.

04
Your Pace

Whether it's a solo study session, a slow weekend brunch, or a catch-up with friends — Ruma 52 holds space for all of it.
